Training
--------

Process
-------

A project owner can be trained by the MEG lab scientists to become an authorized operator.
Over the course of the training sessions, potential users will be taught about the operation protocol described above, the emergency procedures, the safety rules to follow and any
operations that must be done in the lab prior/post data acquisition.

Once the all training sessions are fully completed, the following form should be submitted to the MEG lab scientists.
